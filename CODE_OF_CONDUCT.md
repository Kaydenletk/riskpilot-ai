# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want RiskPilot AI to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level or background.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code,
and may ban contributors for repeated or severe violations. Report issues to the
maintainer via a GitHub issue or by email (see the repo profile).

This is a condensed adaptation of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
